Columbia Missourian: Former MU swimmer Mack Darragh qualifies for 2016 Rio Olympics

While checking this June 13, 2016, article, it came to my attention that swimmer Mack Darragh couldn't have qualified for the 200-meter fly with his time in the trials. I brought it to the reporter's attention and the error was fixed the next day. I also reorganized the story to improve its flow.


Columbia Missourian: Legacy of Stormin' Norman Stewart built on his character, charisma, coaching

I revisited this 2014 Norm Stewart profile as a project to go with an April 30, 2016, story about a new ESPN documentary on the legendary Missouri basketball coach. I went through and made changes to the story, wrote subheads, embedded photos, and chose lines to display as pull quotes to complete the story's longform web presentation.
