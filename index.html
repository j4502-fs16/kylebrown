I am a senior at the University of Misouri-Columbia graduating in December 2016 with a Bachelor's of Journalism in Print and Digital News and a Bachelor's of Arts in International Studies. I have worked at the Columbia Missourian as a copy editor, reporter and page designer, and at Catalan News Agency as a summer intern in 2015. I was admitted to the School of Journalism's Barcelona study abroad program in summer 2015, where I was able to learn about Catalan language, culture and politics, and I built upon that knowledge to present on the Catalan independence movement in the 2016 Symposium on Catalan Language, Literature and Culture at the University of Missouri.
